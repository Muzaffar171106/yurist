Article 1. Relations regulated by the present Code
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
The present Code shall regulate relations related to the preparation and conduct of elections of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, deputies of the Legislative Chamber of the Oliy Majlis of the Republic of Uzbekistan (hereinafter — the Legislative Chamber), members of the Senate of the Oliy Majlis of the Republic of Uzbekistan (hereinafter — the Senate), deputies of regional, sub-regional
1
and city Kengashes of people's deputies (hereinafter — local Kengashes) and define the guarantees that provide for freedom of expression of the citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
1
Sub-region in this case and hereinafter means an administrative unit, which is smaller than a region but bigger than a city. The exact translation for the original Russian word is district. But the translator uses the term “sub-region” to avoid the confusion between the District Election Commissions (DEC) (Translatorʼs notes).
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 2. Legislation on election
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Legislation on election consists of the
Constitution
of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the present Code and other legislative acts of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 3. Basic principles of conducting of elections in the Republic of Uzbekistan
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Elections in the Republic of Uzbekistan shall be held on the basis of universal, equal and direct suffrage by secret ballot.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Elections shall be held open and transparent.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 4. Universal suffrage
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Elections of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, deputies of the Legislative Chamber and deputies of local Kengashes are universal.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan (hereinafter referred to as citizens) who have reached the age of eighteen before election day or on election day have the right to vote.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Citizens, regardless of gender, race and nationality, language, attitude to religion, social origin, beliefs, social status, education, type and nature of occupation, have equal suffrage.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 5. Equal suffrage
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Every citizen participating in the election shall have one vote.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Citizens declared legally incapable by the court, as well as persons being held in a place of detention by order of a court, may not be elected.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Citizens recognized by the court as incompetent, as well as persons held in places of deprivation of liberty by a court verdict for committing grave and especially grave crimes, may be deprived of the right to participate in elections only in accordance with the law and on the basis of a court decision. In any other cases, direct or indirect restriction of the voting rights of citizens is not allowed.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 6. Direct suffrage
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
The President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, deputies of the Legislative Chamber, deputies of the local Kengashes shall be directly elected by citizens.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 7. Secret ballot
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Voting in elections is free and by secret ballot. Control over the will of voters is not allowed.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
The secret ballot shall be ensured by creating appropriate conditions that exclude any possibility of control over the will of the voter.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 8. Openness and transparency in the preparation and conducting of elections
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Election commissions carry out the preparation and conduct of elections in an open and transparent manner.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Election commissions shall inform citizens about their work, formation of election constituencies, precincts, composition of election commissions, their location and working hours, familiarize them with votersʼ lists, list of political parties participating in the elections, provide information on candidates for the Presidency of the Republic of Uzbekistan, deputies of the Legislative Chamber, deputies of the local Kengashes, as well as the results of voting and elections.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Mass media shall cover the process of the preparation and the conduct of the elections.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Meetings of election commissions shall be held open. The decisions of election commissions shall be published in mass media or publicly disclosed in accordance with the procedure established by present Code.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Observers from the political parties which nominated the candidates for the Presidency of the Republic of Uzbekistan, deputies of the Legislative Chamber, deputies of the local Kengashes, from citizensʼ self-governing bodies, representatives of media, overseas observers and observers of the international organizations have the right to be present in all events associated with the preparation and conducting of elections, at voting premises on election day and at the counting of votes.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 8
1
. Restrictions on holding elections
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
During the entire period of the state of emergency imposed on the entire territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan or in its separate locality, elections are not held.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Chapter 2. Constituencies and polling stations
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 9. Formation of election constituencies
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
During the elections of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the entire territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan is a single electoral constituency.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Seventy-five territorial single-member electoral districts are formed for the election of deputies to the Legislative Chamber.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Single-member electoral districts for elections of deputies of the Legislative Chamber are formed by the Central Election Commission of the Republic of Uzbekistan (hereinafter referred to as the Central Election Commission) on the proposal of the Jokargy Kenes of the Republic of Karakalpakstan, the Kengashes of people's deputies of the regions and the city of Tashkent once every five years.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
To hold elections to the Legislative Chamber based on the list of candidates nominated by political parties for deputies of the Legislative Chamber (hereinafter referred to as the party list), the entire territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan is a single electoral district.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
The boundaries of single-member electoral districts for elections of deputies to the Legislative Chamber are determined taking into account the administrative-territorial structure of the Republic of Karakalpakstan, regions and the city of Tashkent, as a rule, with an equal number of voters throughout the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
To hold elections to local Kengashes, electoral districts are formed in an equal number of deputy seats in the regional, Tashkent city, district (city) Kengashes of people's deputies.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
In elections held to the Legislative Chamber and local Kengashes in a single-member electoral district, one deputy is elected from each electoral district.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
The number of electoral districts for elections to the regional Kengash of people's deputies of a newly formed region is established by the Senate, and the number of electoral districts for elections to the district, city Kengash of people's deputies of a newly formed region, city is established by the corresponding regional, Tashkent city Kengash of people's deputies, taking into account the requirements of Article 88 of this Code.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Electoral districts for elections to the regional Kengash of people's deputies of the newly formed region are formed by the Central Election Commission on the proposal of the Senate, and electoral districts for elections to the district, city Kengash of people's deputies of the newly formed region, city - by the corresponding territorial election commission of the region and the city of Tashkent (hereinafter - territorial election commission) on the proposal of the corresponding regional, Tashkent city Kengash of people's deputies.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Single-member constituencies for elections to local Kengashes are formed by the corresponding territorial, district, city election commission, as a rule, with an equal number of voters once every five years. The boundaries of electoral districts are determined taking into account the administrative-territorial structure of the region, districts and cities.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
When forming single-member electoral districts, the maximum allowable deviation of the number of voters in electoral districts, as a rule, should not exceed ten percent.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Lists of single-member electoral districts indicating their boundaries, the number of voters and the locations of election commissions are published by the relevant election commission at least seventy days before the election.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Article 10. Procedure and norm of formation of the polling stations
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Polling stations are formed for a period of five years by territorial election commissions on the representation of district and city khokimiyats. In necessary cases, polling stations may be transformed, the boundaries of existing polling stations may be changed or abolished upon the proposal of district and city khokimiyats.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Polling stations are formed taking into account the boundaries of districts, cities, districts within cities in order to create maximum convenience for voters.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Polling stations can be formed at diplomatic and other missions of the Republic of Uzbekistan in foreign countries, in military units, sanatoriums, rest homes, hospitals and other inpatient medical institutions, in places where citizens are located in remote and hard-to-reach areas, as well as in places of detention and imprisonment during the preparation and conduct of elections. These polling stations are included in the electoral districts at their location. The boundaries of polling stations should not cross the boundaries of other electoral districts.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
In military units, polling stations are formed on the proposal of the commanders of units or military formations, in places of detention and imprisonment, polling stations are formed on the proposal of the heads of these institutions and bodies by territorial election commissions.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
To hold elections of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan and deputies of the Legislative Chamber in a single electoral district on the basis of the party list, the Central Election Commission, on the proposal of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Uzbekistan, may establish polling stations at diplomatic and other missions of the Republic of Uzbekistan in foreign countries. The issue of attaching a polling station formed outside the Republic of Uzbekistan to the corresponding territory shall be decided by the Central Election Commission.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Polling stations shall be formed at least sixty days before the election, usually with a number of at least twenty voters and no more than three thousand voters. In military units, at diplomatic and other missions of the Republic of Uzbekistan in foreign countries, as well as in places where citizens are located in remote and hard-to-reach areas, in places of detention and imprisonment, polling stations shall be formed within the same period, and in exceptional cases — no later than seven days before the elections.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
The number of polling stations cannot be less than the number of districts.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
The Territorial Election Commission sets the numbering of polling stations and organizes notification of voters about the boundaries of each polling station, indicating the phone numbers, the location of the relevant precinct election commission and the voting premises.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Polling stations are single for elections of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, deputies of the Legislative Chamber and deputies of local Kengashes.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш

Article 11. System of election commissions
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
The system of election commissions includes:
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Central Election Commission;
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
territorial election commissions;
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
sub-regional and city election commissions;
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
district election commissions for holding elections of regional and Tashkent city Kengashes of people's deputies (hereinafter referred to as district election commissions);
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
precinct election commissions.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
Ҳужжат элементидан ҳавола олиш
Election commissions and their members shall carry out their activities independently from any state bodies, public associations and officials.
Ҳужжатга таклиф юбориш
Аудиони тинглаш
